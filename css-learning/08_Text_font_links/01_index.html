Typography Matters

The art of arranging type to make written language beautiful

Why Typography is Important

Typography is more than just choosing fonts. It's about creating hierarchy, ensuring readability, and establishing the personality of your content.

Key Principles

  • Choose fonts that complement each other
  • Maintain proper spacing and line height
  • Create clear visual hierarchy
  • Ensure readability across devices
